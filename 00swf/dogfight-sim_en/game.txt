Title: Dogfight SIM

Width: 640

Height: 450

Tags: War, Flight Simulator, Flying

Description:
In Dogfight SIM, its the second World War and you're a lone pilot in the skies. Are you the best there is, born to fight, shoot and kill? Maneuver your plane to avoid enemy fire and mid-air collisions. This is a very fast-paced super War Time Flight Simulator game. Do you have what it takes to rule the skies? Pilots, to your planes!

Instructions:
1) Options are to use the MOUSE to fly + LEFT CLICK click to open fire.
2) Or use the Keyboard ARROW KEYS + CTRL to shoot.
