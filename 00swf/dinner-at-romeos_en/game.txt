Title: Dinner At Romeos

Width: 650

Height: 500

Tags: Food, Business, RPG, Managing

Description:
Dinner at Romeos a restaurant management game, your job is to welcome guests, take their orders and serve their food. Don't let the customers wait too long or they might leave your restaurant! The strategy to this game is just to keep the line moving. Just tend to the customers as they request. Try to remember their orders and deliver them quickly. and keep an eye on your cash too.
