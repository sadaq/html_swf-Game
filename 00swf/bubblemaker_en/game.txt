Description:
In Bubble Maker your aim is to catch as many cherry flowers as possible before time runs out. To do this, you need to make a bubble and keep the flower inside it. You make a bubble by holding down the left mouse button. Avoid catching the bees as they will reduce your score. You will get bonus time by catching the clock symbol. Get a friend and see who can score the highest.
