Title: Boom Boom Bloon

Width: 640

Height: 480

Tags: Shooting

Description:
Boom Boom Bloon! Rescue the good balloon and test your aiming skills to destroy the bad balloons. This game is all about accuracy and precision, make your shot, but make it wisely in this strategy-shooter game! Do you think you have what it takes to rescue the good balloons and destroy the bad balloons? Well good luck! 

Instructions:
Aim and shoot - Use mouse to aim, left click to shoot.
