Title: Counter Terror

Width: 640

Height: 530

Tags: Action, Killing, 2 Player, Adventure, Shoot Em Up, Shooting

Description:
Sneaking through the city's various facilities and taking out terrorists with stealth and skill is what your job is all about. Play alone or with a friend in cooperative mode to clear the terrorists. Use all kinds of cool utilities that come standard for this kind of special-forces work. This arcade-style action-scroller comes packed with interactive environments and tons of tactics to clear each stage.

Instructions:
Cursor keys for movement.
Z = Jump
X = Shoot
C = Deploy special item
S = Switch special item

Player 2 - See in game controls.
