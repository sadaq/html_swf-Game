Title: A.L.I.A.S

Width: 550

Height: 350

Tags: Shooting, Robot, War, Army

Description:
In this action packed multi level game you have to help A.L.I.A.S (Artificial Life-form Intended for Assassination & Sabotage) escape the secret laboratory where he was created. You must help him battle his way through the army base and blast all the soldiers and obstacles with his high fire rate gun. Use W,A,S & D to move around and then when you need to aim and fire you can do so by using the mouse. Each kill restores a bit of health and also power-ups help for that as well.

Instructions:
&middot; Movements = Use W,A,S & D Keys
&middot; Aim and Fire = Use Mouse
