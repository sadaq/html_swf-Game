Title: Arms Dealer

Width: 500

Height: 400

Tags: Business, Gun, RPG, Strategy

Description:
In Arms Dealer, you are a lord of warfare, travelling the globe in search of clients! The aim of the game is to get as much money as you can by dealing weapons. You have 300 days. Buy passports that will give you access to the different areas of the globe, fly around and trade weapons! Be sure to watch the market for price fluctuations as clients declare war on each other and occasionally sign peace treaties!  Good Luck!
